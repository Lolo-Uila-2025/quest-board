Challenge: Movement Alarm

Use an ultrasonic sensor to detect movement. When something comes closer than 20 cm, play an alarm that alternates between two tones five times.

Required Components

  • Arduino board
  • HC‑SR04 ultrasonic sensor
  • Passive buzzer

Wiring

  • TRIG → 12
  • ECHO → 11
  • Buzzer → pin 8 + GND

Logic Hints

  1. Use the SR04 library or your own timing to get distance.
  2. If distance < 20 cm, loop 5 times: 
    tone(8, 1000, 200);
delay(250);
tone(8, 600, 200);
delay(250);

🎯 Goal

Show the alarm sounding exactly five high/low beeps when something moves close to earn +15 points!
