Wiring Instructions

  1. Place the potentiometer on the bridge of the breadboard, the one pin side facing up.
  2. Connect the one pin of the potentiometer to A0 on the Arduino.
  3. Connect one bottom pin to 5 V.
  4. Connect the other bottom pin to GND.
