Quest: Infinite Serial Loop

This quest challenges you to create an **infinite loop** that continuously prints to the Serial Monitor. You'll see how the loop() function runs over and over again forever.

What You’ll Learn

  • How loop() works
  • How to repeatedly output messages using Serial.println()
  • How to slow down an infinite loop with delay()

Instructions

  1. Open the Arduino IDE and create a new sketch.
  2. Inside setup(), begin serial communication: 
    Serial.begin(9600);
  3. Inside loop(), print a message continuously: 
    Serial.println("I'm stuck in a loop!");
  4. Upload your sketch and open the Serial Monitor. You should see your message appearing over and over.

Once your infinite loop works, show your Serial Monitor to the teacher to earn +5 points.
