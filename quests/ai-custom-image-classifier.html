Teachable Machine: Internet Images

In this quest, you’ll use Teachable Machine again — but this time, instead of using your webcam, you’ll train your model using images downloaded from the internet!

🎯 Your Goal

Create a working image classifier using at least 3 different categories of images. Once trained, use the live preview to see if your model can guess correctly from new test images.

📂 Ideas for Categories

  • Different breeds of dogs or cats
  • Different animals (e.g. dolphin, fox, elephant)
  • Logos of different sports teams
  • Different types of food (pizza, burger, ramen, etc.)
  • Screenshots from different video games

🔧 Steps to Follow

  1. Go to teachablemachine.withgoogle.com.
  2. Choose the "Image Project" and then select "Standard image model".
  3. Click “Upload” for each class, and add at least 10–15 pictures per category.
  4. Train your model using the “Train Model” button.
  5. Test it using the “Preview” section.

💡 Tips

  • You can use Google Images to find pictures. Right-click and “Save As...”
  • The more images you add, the smarter your model will be.
  • Make sure your photos clearly show what each category is!

✅ Completion

Show your working model to your instructor or a classmate, and demonstrate how it can tell the difference between your categories. If it can recognize them reliably — even with new images — you’ll earn +10 points!
