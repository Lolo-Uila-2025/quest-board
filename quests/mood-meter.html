Side Quest: Mood Meter

In this version of the quest, you’ll use a potentiometer to “read your mood” and display it in the Serial Monitor. Turning the knob changes the message that appears in the Serial Monitor!

Required Components

  • Arduino board
  • Potentiometer
  • Breadboard & jumper wires

Wiring Tips

Connect the potentiometer like this:

  • Middle pin → A0
  • One side → 5V
  • Other side → GND

🎯 Goal

Turn the knob and watch the Serial Monitor update your mood. Make sure you include at least 4 distinct moods to earn +5 points for completing this quest.
